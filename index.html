Case Study: Brussels Government Website Analysis

See how we transformed complex web traffic data into actionable insights

Interactive Dashboard

Comprehensive analysis of visitor behavior patterns and engagement metrics

The Challenge

The Brussels government needed to understand complex visitor behavior patterns across their website - similar to how Shopify store owners need visibility into their customer journey to optimize conversions.

Our Approach

We applied the same data analysis methodology we use for e-commerce:

  • Cross-platform tracking integration
  • User flow analysis
  • Engagement metrics evaluation
  • Conversion point identification

The Results

  • Discovered that the top three pages account for approximately 38% of total traffic, with clear language preferences (French pages receiving significantly more traffic than Dutch equivalents)
  • Identified distinct engagement patterns across content types, with webcam feeds showing exceptionally high engagement ratios of 55+
  • Found meaningful year-over-year trends showing a 31% decrease in total page views but only an 18% decrease in active users, suggesting improved navigation efficiency
  • Revealed a strong correlation coefficient of 0.92 between views and active users, indicating most pages attract new visitors rather than repeat views
